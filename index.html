Recently I'm practicing this song. I heard it for the first time from the band Sun City Girls, the name was "summer dream(Amherst 2004)" , I looked for the guitar chords but didn't really find it. Then I searched the lyric and just knew it's a song from The Beach Boys.

I prefer the one from Sun City Girls, it was recorded during their live performance probably in a small bar. It must be on a sweet drunk night of summer. People were sweating and singing with full of memories in the mind, love, laughters, loss, tears, and guitars and beats and rebells and sadness and perfect imperfection and regrets and alcohol.
