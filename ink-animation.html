Interactive Ink Animation

Tools:
processing, photoshop, leap motion

 In this project I brought traditional Chinese literati painting to life.
The interactive part starts with reaching a hand upon Leap Motion controller, which can sense the movement of your hands, a frog will appear on the screen and move according to the hand position. All of the tadpoles will swim away from it. When there's no hand, the frog will disappear and the babies will swim back to their original position.
