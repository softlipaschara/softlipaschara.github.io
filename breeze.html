Breeze
Supervised by Prof. Carola Zwick
Semester 5

Tools:
Arduino, Rhino

Starting Point:
I took up analog experiences of light which origins from fire. I want to use digital technologies to make neo-analog experience and gesture.

There are 3 metaphors from the physical world:
 1. When you set a fire, you could blow towards it to make the air flow, the fire will be brighter and stronger.
2. A sudden, strong and quick blow makes the candle out.
3. Like the torch in the Olympia ritual, the flame can be passed one to another spot.
